How to Add This Signature to Gmail

  1. Click on the Generate Signature button to copy the email signature to your clipboard.
  2. Open your Gmail account and go to Settings by clicking the gear icon in the top right corner.
  3. Click on See all settings to open the full settings menu.
  4. Select the Signature tab under the General settings.
  5. If you don’t have a signature set up yet, click Create new and give it a name.
  6. Click on the text area where your signature appears, and paste the copied signature (Ctrl + V or Command + V).
  7. Adjust the formatting if needed and ensure that all images are correctly displayed.
  8. Scroll to the bottom of the settings page and click Save Changes.
  9. Your new email signature is now ready to use!
